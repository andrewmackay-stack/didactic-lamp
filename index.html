Wellbeing Continuum: Underperformance Management

Click on a card to reveal coaching questions.

IN CRISIS

Struggling to cope, physically and mentally exhausted

  • Very anxious
  • Very low mood
  • Absenteeism
  • Exhausted
  • Very poor sleep
  • Weight loss

Coaching Questions:

  • Of everything on your plate, what feels the most daunting?
  • Is there anything outside of work contributing to this feeling?
  • What specific aspects of your work are draining your energy most?
  • What would a small win look like for you right now?
  • Would it be helpful if I shared details for our EAP?

STRUGGLING

Feeling overwhelmed and under pressure

  • Anxious
  • Depressed
  • Tired
  • Poor performance areas
  • Poor sleep
  • Poor appetite

Coaching Questions:

  • Can you walk me through what's on your mind?
  • Is it helpful for me to know if external factors are playing a part?
  • Which task is causing the most pressure?
  • What's one task you feel confident about, and one you feel less certain about?
  • On a scale of 1-10, how in control of your workload do you feel?

SURVIVING

Just getting by, feeling reactive with low energy reserves

  • Worried & Nervous
  • Irritable & Sad
  • Trouble sleeping
  • Distracted
  • Withdrawn

Coaching Questions:

  • What does "just getting by" feel like for you in your day-to-day work?
  • Is it useful for me to be aware of any external factors impacting you?
  • Which parts of your role are manageable, and which feel like a struggle?
  • If you could change one thing about your workflow, what would it be?
  • What is one skill or support that would make your day-to-day feel more achievable?

THRIVING

Feeling engaged and effective, growing and learning

  • Positive & Calm
  • Performing
  • Sleeping well
  • Eating normally
  • Normal social activity

Coaching Questions:

  • What specific elements of your work are making you feel so engaged?
  • How can we ensure you continue to have what you need to sustain this?
  • What are you most passionate about working on right now?
  • Are there any small obstacles we could remove to help you move to excelling?
  • What's one new thing you'd like to learn to keep the momentum going?

EXCELLING

Finding flow and purpose, feeling energised and seeking new challenges

  • Cheerful & Joyful
  • Energetic
  • High performance
  • Flow
  • Realising potential

Coaching Questions:

  • What's making work feel so positive and engaging for you?
  • What's the next challenge or opportunity you'd like to take on?
  • Is there anything you've learned that could benefit the rest of the team?
  • What new skills are you interested in developing to stretch yourself further?
  • Let's talk about your long-term career aspirations.
